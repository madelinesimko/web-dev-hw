Fun Facts

Lake Tahoe is the second deepest lake in the United States.

It is sunny on average 75% of the year.

Two thirds of the lake is in California and the other 1/3 is in Nevada.

There is an average of 274 sunny days a year.

Tahoe is considered one of the 20 oldest lakes in the world.

The lake is incredibly clear; you can see up to 70 ft below the surface.
